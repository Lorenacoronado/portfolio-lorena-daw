About me

Web Application Development (DAW), with a clear goal: becoming a versatile and complete Full-Stack Developer. I am actively seeking my first professional experience to apply my knowledge and grow within the fascinating world of technology.

My passion for IT is vocational and started at the age of 14, exploring web design and building my first computer. This curiosity led me to pursue a Middle Grade in Systems and Networks (SMR), which solidified my technical foundation and confirmed my path toward web development.

What sets me apart is my adaptability and work ethic, forged in demanding international environments such as Amazon in London. Living and working abroad for three years has granted me a fluent and professional level of English, as well as the ability to manage teams and coordinate complex processes in cultural projects.

My main focus areas are:

  • Frontend & UI/UX: Applying my extensive photography and design background to create intuitive, user-friendly, and aesthetic interfaces that enhance the final user experience.
  • Backend & Logic: Highly interested in developing robust business logic and ensuring efficient data management (SQL) through clean and scalable code architectures.
